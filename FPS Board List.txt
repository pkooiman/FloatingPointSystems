Part No. Breakdown
N### = FPS identification number?
     * N612 is used on board stiffener
     * N412 is used on silkscreen
4### = Board series number
     * All boards are either 42##, 43##, 44##, or 48##
###L = Revision number
     * Some boards have just a number, some have the extra letter

There are two different card edge connectors depending on the board
2x 100-pin = 200-pin with 0.125" pitch
2x 120-pin = 240-pin with 0.100" pitch

For clarity, only the Board Series Number will be listed below
